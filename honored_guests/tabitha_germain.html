Tabitha St. Germain

Tabitha St. Germain is an award-winning Canadian stage actress. She began working in animation in Toronto provided the voices for Kootie Pie Koopa in the SUPER MARIO BROS. cartoons, Spryte the fairy in THE LEGEND OF ZELDA, Miss Demeanor in C.O.P.S., and Prudence in the BEETLEJUICE TV series. Since moving to Vancouver, she has voiced many Anime characters, including Gotenks in DRAGON BALL Z , Flay Allister in GUNDAM SEED, Naomi Misura in DEATHNOTE. She appeared as Captain Flamingo in CAPTAIN FLAMINGO, as Martha in Martha Speaks, Heloise in JIMMY-TWO SHOES. She has been a regular in Barbie movies, also LEAGUE OF SUPER EVIL(All the female characters) NERDS AND MONSTERS, LITTLEST PET SHOP, SABRINA: SECRETS OF A TEENAGE WITCH, PAC-MAN, LOLLIROCKS, PIRATE EXPRESS, ENDANGERED SPECIES and many other live-action, cartoon and anime series. All in all, she has appeared in over 300 productions, both on stage and on television.

In MY LITTLE PONY: FRIENDSHIP IS MAGIC, she is the voice of Rarity, Princess Luna, Nightmare Moon, Granny Smith, Mrs. Cake, Photo Finish, Rarity's mother, Derpy, Suri, Pound Cake and others.

Tabitha St. Germain 是一名才华横溢的加拿大籍女演员，她最早在《超级马里奥兄弟》的动画中为库巴军团的温蒂（Wendy O.Koopa），《塞尔达传说》动画里的仙子Spryte等配音。自从她搬到温哥华后，她又配音了一系列的英文版动画角色，包括《龙珠Z》中的悟天克斯，《机动战士高达SEED》中的芙蕾·阿尔斯塔，《死亡笔记》中的南空直美。她也是一系列芭比电影中的芭比配音。她还配音了许多中国观众可能不太熟悉但是在北美非常受欢迎的英文动画，像是《Captain Flamingo》，《Martha Speaks》，《JIMMY-TWO SHOES》，《LEAGUE OF SUPER EVIL》，《NERDS AND MONSTERS》，《LITTLEST PET SHOP》，《SABRINA》，《PAC-MAN》，《LOLLIROCKS》，《PIRATE EXPRESS》，《ENDANGERED SPECIES》和许多其他的卡通和动画作品。总结来说，Tabitha St. Germain 在舞台上和电视上一共配音了超过 300 多部作品。

在小马宝莉中，她配音了瑞瑞、露娜、梦魇之月、史密斯婆婆、蛋糕太太、瑞瑞的妈妈、小呆、苏蕊、蛋糕夫妇的女儿和其他角色。
