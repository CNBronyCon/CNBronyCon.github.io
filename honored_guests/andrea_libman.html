Andrea Libman

Andrea Libman has worked as a voice over actor for more than twenty years. She has had roles on numerous animated TV series, including Madeline in Madeline and The New Adventures of Madeline, Emmy in DragonTales, young Andrea in Reboot, and Maya in Maya the Bee. Recently, she's played Katya on Super Monsters, and Lemon Meringue, Pupcake and Princess Berrykin in Strawberry Shortcake's Berry Bitty Adventures. Andrea is the two-time recipient of the Union of BC Performers Award for Best Voice for her portrayal of Pinkie Pie and Fluttershy in My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic.

Andrea teaches and plays the piano and enjoys running, skiing and snowboarding, yoga and volunteer work. She holds her Bachelor of Applied Science in Civil Engineering from the University of British Columbia.

Andrea Libman 从 6 岁就开始了她的配音生涯，到现在已经二十多年了。她最出名的角色是获得过艾美奖的动画《玛德琳》中的玛德琳，以及《小马宝莉》中的萍琪派和小蝶。 Andrea的配音作品还包括《蜜蜂玛雅》，《草莓女孩》等等。最近她配音了《吃豆人的鬼魅历险》和《超级怪兽》。

Andrea 学习并教授钢琴，而且拥有不列颠哥伦比亚大学颁发的土木工程应用学学士学位。为萍琪派和小蝶配音让她获得了两次不列颠哥伦比亚大学表演联合会的最佳配音奖。
