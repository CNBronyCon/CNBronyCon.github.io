Frequently Asked Question

What is CNBronyCon?

CNBronyCon is a non-official, non-profit convention operated by Chinese bronies for Chinese fans of “My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic”, that aims to provide a platform for Chinese bronies to communicate with each other in real life. Foreign bronies are also welcome in the convention.

When and where will CNBronyCon 2018 be held?

CNBronyCon 2018 will be held in Dahongmen International Convention & Exhibition Center, Beijing, between 14 and 15 July, 2018.

To get you notified the evening before the conference starts, please download this calendar event (in iCalendar format) and import it into your own calendar account.

How should I get to the convention?

Please refer to the Traffic Information for public transportation information.

What do I need to do after I get to the convention?

You may want to register at the ticket office and enter the convention hall to begin your tour.

Can I join the convention if I am still a child?

It is strongly adviced that you have your parents or guardians with you when you are in the convention hall, if you are below 18 years old.

What exactly happens at CNBronyCon? What is there to do?

Events that have been planned to run on CNBronyCon includes meeting with voice actors, DJ night and Fighting is Magic.

Unfortuantely, you will not be able to submit your own events because it has met its deadline on 30 April, 2018.

Is cosplay allowed at the convention?

Cosplay of any type is welcome at CNBronyCon, with restrictions of the following:

  • Cosplays that are not suitable for work (NSFW), are strictly prohibitted in the convention hall;
  • Cosplays that have political metaphors, or insult present or past presidents of Chinese government, are strictly prohibitted in the convention hall.
  • Cosplays that have weapons, or accessories that may hurt other persons are strictly prohibitted in the convention hall.

Meanwhile, we are holding a contest over My Little Pony character cosplays. Anyone that has passed the first stage of the contest will receive a Cosplayer's Pass from the Committee and can come to the convention without the need to purchase a ticket. To enroll in the contest, upload a picture of your cosplay to an image hosting website (i.e. Imgur and Simple Free Image Hosting) and send the link of your corresponding picture to our mailbox.

Cosplayer that wins the final stage of the contest will get a free plushie. Cosplayers that have not passed the first stage (cosplays that does not meet the minimum requirements of this contest, or is a cosplay of origianal character (OC) or non-My-Little-Pony characters will not pass the first stage) will have to purchase tickets to enter the conference hall, will not be able to receive a Cosplayer's Pass and will not be able to participate future stages of the contest.

The submit deadline for this contest is 30 June, 2018.

I want to help out! How do I volunteer for CNBronyCon?

While we appreciate it that you even have the thought on helping us out, we currently have no plan to accept foreign volunteers.

Can I play PMVs that I have made or other MLP related videos on the screen in the middle of the convention hall?

However, if you are interested, we are running a contest over videos on our moscots. If you could produce a video on our moscot, we would consider setting your video as our opening sequence video. You will be awarded CNY ¥1,666.00 if your work get picked by us.

To enroll, upload your work to YouTube and send a link of your work to our mailbox, or contact our staff LA-ndy via QQ.

The submit deadline for this contest is 30 June, 2018.
