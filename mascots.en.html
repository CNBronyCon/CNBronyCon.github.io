Mascots

CNBronyCon this year brought three new friends to you!

Here welcome Harmonia, Dance Lion and Winter Sweet! The three little ponies with prominent Chinese characteristics carry forward together love and tolerance all over the world. Harmonia, who is calm and tranquil, Dance Lion being outgoing and enthusiastic, together with such a sweetie as Winter Sweet all desperate to introduce themselves!

Harmonia 睦睦

Harmonia, the dragon-pony of peace and harmony comes from the Celestial Temple (heaven). She cares for the lives in all lands beneath and dreams to help them. The Jade Emperor (the Supreme Deity of Heaven) knew her will and sent her down to the civilized mortal land studying the magic of friendship, as well as helping mortals (people) gain prosperity and happiness. Harmonia got the chance to know Dance Lion and Winter Sweet in the Chinese land and soon became best friends with them. From then on, Harmonia started the amazing quest for friendship and new friends.

Dance Lion 狮狮

Dance Lion is a passionate earth pony. He is extroverted straightforward. He likes spicy Szechuan food, especially hot pot. In festival season, he brought Harmonia together with him to experience his favorite splendid traditional Chinese festival celebration activities, like the Lion Dance and Dragon Boat racing.

Winter Sweet 小梅

Winter Sweet is a sweet little Pegasus. She loves wearing Hanfu and Cheongsam. She's an expert in traditional Chinese arts, including martial arts, which literally startled Harmonia with her amazing Kung Fu skills. She was timid meeting Harmonia for the first time, but after knowing better about each other, Winter Sweet shows her energy and vivaciousness.

Those above are our new friends in Beijing CNBC! They are looking forward to meeting you in Beijing!

We also encourage all fellow bronies to show our new friends your acceptance and love in all kinds of ways.
